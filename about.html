ABOUT EXCEED SOLUTIONS


Exceed Solutions is a Tanzanian company that empowers the current generation in running their businesses well and helping them ovecome all kinds of obstacles.At Exceed Company we give the skills and platform to become an effective business person/entreprenuer,we provide business lessons and monitor the business performance.We focus on both large and small businesses ,as our mission is to reduce economic gap btn africa and other continents and empower more sucessful entreprenuer in Tanzania and across Africa.

Exceed Solutions overaching objective is to empower both small and large entreprenuers all over africa.Inorder to achieve this goal Exceed Solutions is implementing an innovative cyclical process of business training and mentorship,notably through the creation of business camps,online and offline business mentorship to match emerging businesses with mentors.

OUR VISION

To see both small and large businesses flourishing and being able to create the kind of income that they want.

OUR MISSION

To give entreprenuers the skills and tools that they need to become successful and the driving change in their communities.
